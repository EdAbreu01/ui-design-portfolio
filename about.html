I'm Ed Vanderhorst,
bridging design and code.

I'm a UI designer with front-end development skills. I focus on designing clear, functional interfaces and bringing them to life with modern web technologies.

My work sits at the intersection of visual design, systems thinking and digital interfaces. With more than a decade of experience in graphic design and advertising, I bring a strong visual language into UI design — where typography, layout and interaction shape how users experience a product. I believe the best interfaces are the ones that feel effortless: clear, intentional and visually coherent. By designing and coding my own interfaces, I aim to close the gap between design thinking and real implementation.
